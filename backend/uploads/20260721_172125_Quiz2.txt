Q1. What will be the output of the following pseudocode?

Integer p = 1

For(each i from 1 to 5)
    If(i % 2 == 0)
        Continue
    End If
    p = p * i
End For

Print p

   A) 15
   B) 12
   C) 20
   D) 30

Answer: A
